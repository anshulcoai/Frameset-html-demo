Places to Visit in Kerala

Introduction

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is a beautiful state in southern India, famous for its lush landscapes, backwaters, beaches, and rich culture. Here are some must-visit places in Kerala: 1. Alleppey (Alappuzha)
Famous for its backwaters, houseboat cruises, and serene waterways.
Visit the Alleppey Beach and enjoy the picturesque sunset.
2. Munnar
A popular hill station known for its tea plantations and rolling green hills.
Explore Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.
Visit the Tea Museum and learn about tea processing.
3. Kochi (Cochin)
A vibrant port city blending modernity and tradition.
Visit the Fort Kochi area to see the Chinese Fishing Nets and St. Francis Church.
Explore the Jewish Synagogue and Mattancherry Palace.
4. Thekkady
Known for Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, famous for its elephants and wildlife safaris.
Enjoy boat rides on Periyar Lake surrounded by forests.
5. Kumarakom
A peaceful backwater village ideal for houseboat cruises and birdwatching.
Visit the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary and spot migratory birds.
6. Wayanad
A lush, green district known for its waterfalls, caves, and wildlife.
Visit Edakkal Caves, known for ancient petroglyphs, and Soochipara Waterfalls.
7. Kovalam
Famous for its stunning beaches, especially Lighthouse Beach and Hawa Beach.
Enjoy water sports like surfing and swimming.
8. Varkala
Known for its cliffside beaches and natural beauty.
Visit the Varkala Beach and take a dip in its therapeutic waters.
Explore the Janardhana Swamy Temple located nearby.
9. Thrissur
Famous for the Thrissur Pooram, one of Kerala's largest temple festivals.
Visit the Vadakkumnathan Temple, a historic and spiritual center.
10. Bekal
Known for the Bekal Fort, offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.
Visit the Bekal Beach for a relaxing experience by the sea.
11. Poovar Island
A secluded and peaceful spot for those looking for serenity.
Enjoy boat rides and witness the meeting point of the backwaters, beach, and sea.

Kerala offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, wildlife, culture, and tranquility, making it an unforgettable destination for travelers.
